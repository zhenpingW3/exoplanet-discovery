EXOPLANET VISUALIZATION - QUICK START

How to use:
1. Open index.html in your web browser
   - Just double-click the file, or
   - Open it in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge

2. Two views to explore:
   - Habitability Funnel: "How many planets could support life?"
   - Discovery Deep Dive: "How and why did we find what we found?"

3. Interact:
   - Click tabs at the top to switch between views
   - Click funnel levels to see more details
   - Click planet types to see example planets
   - Hover for tooltips with additional information

4. What you'll learn:
   - Only 6 planets are both habitable-temperature AND Earth-sized (0.51%)
   - Transit method dominates discoveries (96%) - this biases our sample
   - Hot Jupiters are overrepresented (easy to detect, not representative)
   - Discovery acceleration: 95% found after 2009

Troubleshooting:
- Make sure index.html and data.json are in the SAME folder
- Try a different browser if charts don't load
- Press F12 to open browser console for error messages
- Check internet connection (needs to load Plotly.js from CDN)

Data source: NASA Exoplanet Archive (1,174 planets discovered 2002-2025)
